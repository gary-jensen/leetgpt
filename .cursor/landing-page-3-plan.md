# LeetGPT Landing Page 3 - High-Converting SaaS Structure

**Tone:** Professional, conversion-focused, trust-building
**Focus:** Clear value proposition, social proof, structured benefits, conversion optimization
**Structure:** Based on proven SaaS landing page anatomy

---

## 1. NAVBAR (Sticky)
**Implementation:**
- Make navbar sticky so it's visible at all times
- Logo (LeetGPT) on left
- Navigation links: "Features", "How it works", "Testimonials", "Pricing", "FAQ"
- CTA button: "Get Started" (or "Log in" if authenticated)
- Clean, minimal design with backdrop blur

**Purpose:** Always accessible navigation and CTA

---

## 2. HERO AREA
**Layout:** Split layout - Left: Content, Right: Visual

**Left Side:**
- **Social Proof Badge** (top): "1,200+ developers practicing daily" with small icons
  - Builds trust by showing others are using the tool
- **Main Headline:** 
  - "Master Algorithms with AI-Powered Guidance"
  - Or: "The Algorithm Practice Platform Built for Developers"
  - Clear, benefit-focused
- **Subheadline:**
  - "Practice coding problems, get instant feedback, and ace technical interviews. Join thousands of developers leveling up their problem-solving skills."
- **Primary CTA:** "Start Practicing Free" (orange button)
- **Secondary CTA:** "Watch Demo" or "Try a Problem" (outlined button)
- **Trust Indicators:** "No credit card required" or "Free forever plan available"

**Right Side:**
- **Product Visual:** 
  - Screenshot or video of the algorithm workspace
  - Show: Problem statement, code editor, test results, AI chat
  - Animated or static hero image
  - Could be a GIF showing the workflow

**Annotations:**
- Social proof builds trust
- Clear problem/solution statement
- Multiple CTAs for different user intents

## 8. FAQ SECTION
**Title:** "Frequently Asked Questions"

**Layout:** Accordion-style questions

**Questions to Address:**
1. **Do I need a CS degree?**
   - Answer: No. LeetGPT is designed for self-taught developers and anyone who wants to learn algorithms.

2. **Will you give me the answers?**
   - Answer: No. We provide hints and guidance to help you think through problems, but you'll solve them yourself.

3. **Is it beginner-friendly?**
   - Answer: Yes, but you'll still do the work. We explain concepts simply, but you need to practice.

4. **Do I need an account?**
   - Answer: No. You can try problems without signing up. An account lets you track progress and access more features.

5. **Can this help with interviews?**
   - Answer: Yes. Our problems are similar to what companies ask in technical interviews, and our AI helps you think like an interviewer.

6. **What's the difference between Free and Pro?**
   - Answer: Free includes 5 problems per day and basic hints. Pro gives unlimited problems, advanced AI guidance, and detailed analytics.

7. **Can I cancel anytime?**
   - Answer: Yes. Cancel anytime with no questions asked. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

8. **Do you offer refunds?**
   - Answer: Yes. If you're not satisfied within 30 days, we'll refund your payment.

**Design:**
- Accordion-style (expandable)
- Address major questions to help final decision
- Include cancellation/refund questions
- Clear, simple answers
